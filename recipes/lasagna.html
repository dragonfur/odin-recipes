Lasagna

This is a picture of a freshly cooked lasagna with a cheesy top and a meaty center.

This is a simple recipe that gives you a great lasagna, and is great with salad and garlic bread. It yields 12 servings if you follow the exact ingredient list.

Kristy's lasagna can be used for a quick dinner, and gives you a great meal with cheesy toppings, a meaty center, and layered with lasagna noodles.

    Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 5 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
